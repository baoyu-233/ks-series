# Economy Domain Map

> [中文](domains.md) | English

The economy is divided into market liquidity, official buyback, bank money supply, enterprise production, tax sinks,
property ownership, dungeons, politics, blind boxes, limited sales, and compensation. Each domain has one settlement
owner and calls shared currency/item services instead of duplicating balances.

Blind boxes are weighted random rewards with pity and NBT-preserving delivery. Limited sales are time-and-stock
campaigns with player caps. Compensation is guaranteed recovery delivery and is therefore tracked separately from both
random rewards and scheduled stock.
