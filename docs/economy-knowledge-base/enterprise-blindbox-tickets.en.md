# Enterprise Blind-Box Ticket Audit

> [中文](enterprise-blindbox-tickets.md) | English

This record audits enterprise-funded blind-box tickets. It distinguishes the ticket item, the enterprise public
account, the blind-box pool, player redemption, and storage fallback. A ticket must not silently become a personal
currency balance or bypass configured pool weights and pity rules.

Before enabling the feature, verify ownership, duplicate redemption, expiry, refund, full-inventory delivery, and
auditability against the current schema and live server.
