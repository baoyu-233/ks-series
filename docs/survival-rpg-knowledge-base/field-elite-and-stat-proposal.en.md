# Field Elite and First-Wave Equipment Proposal

> [中文](field-elite-and-stat-proposal.md) | English

This proposal selects the verified `pet_ice_dragon` model for a low-frequency Frostwaste Whelp field elite and
recommends mixed material and starter-weapon-cache drops. It records first-pass full-enchanted-netherite-or-better
combat stats and skill targets.

The proposal is not a live acceptance record. Random spawn behavior, MMOInventory reflection compatibility, and
balance require a post-restart in-game test. `ks-RPG` owns generic weapon/talisman skills, accessory passives,
cache opening, and configured scoreboard-tagged drops; MMOItems and MythicMobs own the content definitions.
