# Server Vision

> [中文](vision.zh-CN.md) | English

## Player Promise

ks-Series is a long-running survival server where a new player can enjoy a recognizable vanilla
survival life, while long-term players gradually discover an RPG world with professions, specialized
equipment, exploration objectives, coordinated bosses, and an economy that makes those activities matter.

## Design Pillars

1. Survival remains the foundation. Building, farming, gathering, trading, and social play must be
   worthwhile without joining a class or clearing an instance.
2. RPG depth is layered, not imposed. Each new system adds a goal or choice to an existing survival
   loop instead of replacing it with menu-driven grind.
3. Cooperation creates advantages, not mandatory attendance. Solo players retain steady progress;
   groups gain access to faster, riskier, or more specialized rewards.
4. The economy is a content system. Material sinks, crafted equipment, repair, land, and rewards must
   form a controlled loop rather than creating unbounded currency or item inflation.
5. Difficulty is readable. Bosses and dangerous areas use visible telegraphs, recoverable failures,
   and rewards that explain why players accepted the risk.

## Non-Goals

- Do not turn the server into a mandatory daily-task MMO.
- Do not invalidate vanilla equipment, farms, or building projects in one update.
- Do not introduce gear-score gates before exploration and crafting provide credible paths to upgrade.
- Do not add a feature solely because a plugin supports it; every system needs a survival-loop purpose.
