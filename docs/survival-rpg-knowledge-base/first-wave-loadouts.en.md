# First-Wave RPG Loadouts

> [中文](first-wave-loadouts.md) | English

The first dungeon prelude uses one weapon, two different rings, and one talisman. Normal survival armor and supplies
remain unrestricted; only the RPG loadout is locked for the instance. The design includes four weapon directions,
six rings, and four talismans and targets a full-enchanted-netherite baseline or higher.

Combat proofs and account-bound progression cannot be purchased, traded, or obtained from blind boxes. The catalog is
specified for the first wave and still needs live combat-stat validation.
