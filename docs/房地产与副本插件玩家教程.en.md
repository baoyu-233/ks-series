# Real-Estate and Dungeon Player Guide

> [中文](房地产与副本插件玩家教程.md) | English

Use the land entry point to inspect plots, homes, ownership, and trusted players. Use the dungeon entry point to form a
party, pay a configured ticket, enter an instance, use paid revives when enabled, and collect completion rewards.

Property ownership and dungeon instances share the economy contract. A full inventory sends rewards to `/storage`.
Tickets, revives, and purchased preparation are economy sinks; key combat proofs are earned from the encounter and are
not intended to be tradable or blind-box rewards.
