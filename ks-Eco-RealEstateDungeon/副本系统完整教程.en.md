# ks-Eco-RealEstateDungeon - Complete Dungeon Guide

> [中文](副本系统完整教程.md) | English

This Extra module adds party-based dungeon instances to the real-estate economy. The dungeon flow includes a lobby,
party membership, leader-paid tickets, isolated void-world grids, MythicMobs encounter tracking, paid revives, property
integration, and JSON-configured completion rewards.

## Player Flow

1. Open `/dungeon` and create or join a party.
2. The party leader confirms the configured ticket payment.
3. Enter the allocated instance and complete the encounter.
4. Use a paid revive when enabled and permitted.
5. Receive completion rewards or recover overflow through `/storage`.

The module shares plot ownership with `ks-Eco-RealEstate`. Combat proofs and RPG breakthroughs are account-bound and
must come from the encounter; they are not ordinary trade goods or blind-box rewards. The module is loaded from
`plugins/ks-Eco/extra/` and is not installed as a top-level Bukkit plugin.

For operator APIs, configuration, instance cleanup, and deployment constraints, see the [full technical report](../docs/KS-SERIES-REPORT.en.md).
